## Getting Started

### Accessing the Application

**Description:** Launch the Wanderlust platform in your web browser.

**How to use:**
1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
2. Navigate to the application URL
3. The home page will load, showing the search hub and featured destinations

**Note:** The application supports multiple languages (English, Vietnamese, Japanese, Korean). You can change the language using the language selector in the header.

## Authentication

### Logging In

**Description:** Sign in to access personalized features, booking history, and saved items.

**How to use:**
1. Click the "Login" or "Sign In" button in the header
2. Enter your email address in the "Email" field
3. Enter your password in the "Password" field
4. Click the eye icon to show/hide your password
5. Click the "Sign In" button to log in

**Alternative Login Methods:**
- Click the "Continue with Google" button to log in using your Google account
- Click the "Continue with Facebook" button to log in using your Facebook account

**Note:** 
- If your email is already registered via social login, you'll need to enter your password to link accounts
- Invalid credentials will show an error message

### Creating an Account

**Description:** Register a new account to start booking services.

**How to use:**
1. On the login page, click "Create account" or "Sign Up"
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Email:** Enter a valid email address
   - **Password:** Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Confirm Password:** Re-enter your password
   - **First Name:** Enter your first name
   - **Last Name:** Enter your last name
   - **Gender:** Select your gender from the dropdown
   - **Mobile:** Enter your phone number
   - **Date of Birth:** Click the calendar icon and select your birth date
   - **Address:** Enter your address
   - **City:** Enter your city
   - **Country:** Enter your country
3. Click the "Sign Up" button to create your account

**Note:** 
- All fields are required for successful registration
- Password must match in both password fields
- Email must be unique and not already registered

### Logging Out

**Description:** Sign out of your account safely.

**How to use:**
1. Click on your profile icon/name in the header
2. Click the "Logout" button from the dropdown menu

## Home Page & Search

### Using the Hero Search Hub

**Description:** Quick search functionality for all travel services from the home page.

**How to use:**

#### Searching for Flights:
1. On the home page, ensure the "Flights" tab is selected (plane icon)
2. Select trip type:
   - Click "One-way" for single direction flights
   - Click "Round-trip" for return flights
   - Click "Multi-city" for multiple destinations
3. Click the "From" field and select departure airport
4. Click the "To" field and select arrival airport
5. Click the swap icon to exchange departure and arrival locations
6. Click the departure date field and select your travel date from the calendar
7. For round-trip, select return date
8. Click the passengers field to specify:
   - Number of adults (use +/- buttons)
   - Number of children (use +/- buttons)
   - Number of infants (use +/- buttons)
   - Cabin class (Economy, Business, First)
9. Click the "Search" button to find available flights

#### Searching for Hotels:
1. Select the "Hotels" tab (hotel icon)
2. Click the destination field and select your destination city
3. Click the "Check-in" date field and select arrival date
4. Click the "Check-out" date field and select departure date
5. Click the guests field to specify:
   - Number of adults (use +/- buttons)
   - Number of children (use +/- buttons)
   - Number of rooms (use +/- buttons)
6. Click the "Search" button to find available hotels

#### Searching for Car Rental:
1. Select the "Car Rental" tab (car icon)
2. Click the pickup location field and select location
3. Click the pickup date field and select date
4. Select pickup time from the dropdown
5. Check "Return to same location" if dropoff is at the same place
6. If different location, click dropoff location field and select
7. Click dropoff date and select return date
8. Select dropoff time
9. Toggle "With Driver" option if needed
10. Click the "Search" button to find available vehicles

#### Searching for Activities:
1. Select the "Activities" tab (party popper icon)
2. Click the location field and select destination
3. Click the date field and select activity date
4. Click the guests field to specify adults and children
5. Click the "Search" button to find available activities

### Browsing Featured Destinations

**Description:** Explore popular travel destinations showcased on the home page.

**How to use:**
1. Scroll down to the "Popular Destinations" section
2. View destination cards showing location images and names
3. Use the left/right arrow buttons to navigate through pages
4. Click on any destination card to view detailed information about hotels, activities, and attractions in that location

### Viewing Top Activities

**Description:** See the highest-rated activities on the home page.

**How to use:**
1. Scroll to the "Top Rated Activities" section
2. Review activity cards showing:
   - Activity name and image
   - Price information
   - Star rating
   - Location
3. Click on any activity card to view full details and book

## Flight Booking

### Searching for Flights

**Description:** Find available flights based on your travel requirements.

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to the Flights page (click "Flights" in the header or use home search)
2. Configure your search using the search form:
   - Select trip type (One-way, Round-trip, Multi-city)
   - Choose departure airport (From field)
   - Choose arrival airport (To field)
   - Select travel dates
   - Specify number of passengers (Adults, Children, Infants)
   - Select cabin class (Economy, Business, First)
3. Click the "Search Flights" button
4. Browse the list of available flights showing:
   - Airline information
   - Departure and arrival times
   - Flight duration
   - Number of stops
   - Price per passenger
5. Use filters to narrow results (if available)

**Note:** Round-trip bookings may require selecting outbound and inbound flights separately.

### Viewing Flight Details

**Description:** See comprehensive information about a specific flight.

**How to use:**
1. From the search results, click on a flight card or "View Details" button
2. Review flight information:
   - Complete flight schedule
   - Baggage allowance
   - Fare rules and restrictions
   - Cancellation policy
   - Available amenities
3. Check seat availability for your selected class
4. Compare different fare options if available

### Selecting Seats

**Description:** Choose your preferred seats on the aircraft.

**How to use:**
1. After selecting a flight, click "Continue" or "Select Seats"
2. View the seat map showing the aircraft layout
3. Different colors indicate:
   - Green: Available seats
   - Grey: Occupied seats
   - Blue: Your selected seats
   - Yellow: Premium seats (may have extra cost)
4. Click on available seats to select them
5. Select seats for all passengers in your booking
6. Review seat selection summary
7. Click "Continue" to proceed to passenger details

**Note:** 
- Some seats may require additional payment
- Not all flights offer advance seat selection
- Exit row seats may have age/health restrictions

### Booking a Flight

**Description:** Complete your flight reservation by entering passenger information and payment.

**How to use:**
1. After selecting seats, you'll be directed to the Booking Details page
2. Fill in passenger information for each traveler:
   - Select title (Mr., Mrs., Ms., etc.)
   - Enter first name
   - Enter middle name (optional)
   - Enter last name
   - Click the date of birth calendar and select birth date
   - Enter frequent flyer program number (optional)
3. Repeat for all passengers (adults, children, infants)
4. Enter contact information:
   - Email address for booking confirmation
   - Phone type (Personal/Business)
   - Country code (default +84)
   - Phone number
5. Review the booking summary showing:
   - Flight details
   - Passenger count
   - Price breakdown (base fare, taxes, fees)
   - Total amount
6. Check the box to agree to terms and conditions (required)
7. Check the marketing communications box (optional)
8. Click "Continue to Payment" button

**Note:**
- All required fields must be filled
- Email and phone number must be valid
- Terms and conditions must be accepted to proceed

### Applying Vouchers to Flights

**Description:** Use promotional codes to get discounts on your flight booking.

**How to use:**
1. On the Flights page, scroll to the "Flight Promotions" section
2. Browse available vouchers showing:
   - Voucher code
   - Discount amount or percentage
   - Validity dates
   - Terms and conditions
3. Click "Save Voucher" to add it to your account (requires login)
4. Click the copy icon to copy the voucher code
5. During booking, enter or select the voucher code in the payment section
6. The discount will be automatically applied to your total

**Note:**
- You must be logged in to save vouchers
- Some vouchers have minimum purchase requirements
- Only one voucher can be used per booking
- Expired vouchers cannot be used

### Reviewing Flight Bookings

**Description:** View and manage your completed and upcoming flight reservations.

**How to use:**
1. After completing a flight booking, you'll see a confirmation page with:
   - Booking reference number
   - Flight details
   - Passenger information
   - Payment confirmation
2. Check your email for the booking confirmation and e-ticket
3. To view later, go to Profile > Booking History
4. Download or print your e-ticket using the download button

### Filtering Hotels

1. On the Hotel List page, use the left sidebar filters:

   - **Star Rating:** Click stars to filter by hotel rating (1-5 stars)

   - **Meal Options:** Choose breakfast inclusion preferences
   - **Guest Rating:** Filter by customer review scores

2. Click "Apply Filters" or filters apply automatically
3. Click "Clear Filters" to reset all filters
4. Results update automatically based on selected filters

### Viewing Hotel Details

**Description:** See complete information about a specific hotel property.

**How to use:**
1. From search results, click on a hotel card
2. View the hotel detail page showing:
   - Photo gallery (click images or use arrows to navigate)
   - Hotel description and overview
   - Exact location with map
   - List of all amenities and facilities
   - Room types and availability
   - Guest reviews and ratings
   - Nearby attractions
   - Hotel policies (check-in/out times, cancellation policy)
3. Scroll through different sections using the page navigation
4. Click the heart icon to save the hotel to your favorites

### Booking a Hotel Room

**Description:** Reserve accommodation at your chosen hotel.

**How to use:**
1. On the hotel detail page, browse available room types
2. Each room shows:
   - Room name and type
   - Maximum occupancy
   - Bed configuration
   - Room amenities
   - Meal options (Room only, Breakfast included, etc.)
   - Cancellation policy
   - Price per night
3. Click "Select" on your preferred room type
4. Review booking summary on the right showing:
   - Hotel name and address
   - Check-in and check-out dates
   - Number of nights
   - Number of guests
   - Room type selected
   - Price breakdown
5. Click "Continue to Book" or "Book Now"
6. Fill in guest information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Special requests (optional)
7. Review and accept hotel policies
8. Click "Proceed to Payment"

**Note:**
- Prices may vary based on season and availability
- Some rates may be non-refundable
- Check cancellation policy before booking

### Writing Hotel Reviews

**Description:** Share your experience after staying at a hotel.

**How to use:**
1. Go to Profile > Booking History
2. Find your completed hotel booking
3. Click "Write Review" button
4. In the review dialog:

     - Amenities

     - Value for money

## Car Rental

### Searching for Rental Cars

**Description:** Find vehicles available for rent at your destination.

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to the Car Rental page
2. Use the search form:
   - Click the pickup location field and select a location
   - Click the pickup date field and select date from calendar
   - Select pickup time from the time dropdown
   - Check "Return to same location" if you're returning the car to the same place

   - Toggle "With Driver" if you want a car with a driver
3. Click the "Search" button
4. Browse available vehicles showing:
   - Vehicle image
   - Car model and type
   - Passenger capacity
   - Fuel type (Petrol/Diesel/Electric)
   - Transmission type (Automatic/Manual)

**Description:** Refine search results to find your ideal vehicle.

**How to use:**
1. On the Car Rental List page, use the left sidebar:
   - **Car Type:** Select vehicle categories (Sedan, SUV, Van, Luxury, Electric, etc.)

   - **Price Range:** Drag the slider to set your budget
   - **Transmission:** Filter by Automatic or Manual
   - **Fuel Type:** Select Petrol, Diesel, or Electric
2. Use the sort dropdown to order results by:
   - Most Popular
   - Price: Low to High
   - Price: High to Low
   - Newest First
3. Results update automatically based on filters

### Booking a Rental Car

**Description:** Reserve a vehicle for your travel dates.

**How to use:**
1. From the car listing, click "View Details" on your preferred vehicle
2. Review the car detail page showing:
   - Complete vehicle specifications
   - Included features and services

   - Insurance options
   - Fuel policy
   - Mileage limits
3. Check the rental summary:

   - Dropoff location, date, and time
   - Number of rental days
   - Price breakdown (base rate, taxes, fees)
4. Click "Book Now" or "Reserve"
5. Fill in driver information:
   - Full name as on driver's license
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Driver's license number
   - License issue country
6. Add optional extras:
   - Additional driver
   - GPS navigation system
   - Child safety seat
   - Additional insurance coverage
7. Review terms and conditions
8. Click "Continue to Payment"

**Note:**
- Valid driver's license is required
- Minimum age requirements may apply (usually 21-25 years)

- Check fuel policy (Full-to-Full, Same-to-Same, etc.)

**How to use:**

## Visa Application

### Starting a Visa Application

**Description:** Apply for a travel visa through the platform.

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to the Visa section from the header menu
2. Browse visa options by country
3. Click on your destination country
4. Review visa requirements and information:
   - Visa type available
   - Processing time
   - Validity period
   - Required documents
   - Fees
5. Click "Apply Now" to start the application

### Filling Visa Application Form

**Description:** Complete the multi-step visa application process.

**How to use:**

**Step 1: Personal Information**
1. Enter your full name (as on passport)
2. Click date of birth calendar and select your birth date
3. Enter place of birth
4. Select nationality from dropdown
5. Select gender (Male/Female/Other)
6. Select marital status
7. Click "Next" to continue

**Step 2: Contact Information**
1. Enter email address
2. Enter phone number with country code
3. Enter current address
4. Enter city
5. Click "Next"

**Step 3: Passport Information**
1. Enter passport number
2. Select passport issue date
3. Select passport expiry date
4. Enter place of issue
5. Ensure passport is valid for at least 6 months from travel date
6. Click "Next"

**Step 4: Travel Information**
1. Select purpose of travel (Tourism, Business, Education, etc.)
2. Select intended departure date
3. Select return date
4. Enter accommodation address at destination
5. Click "Next"

**Step 5: Employment Information**
1. Enter your occupation
2. Enter company name (if employed)
3. Enter company address
4. Enter approximate monthly income
5. Click "Next"

**Step 6: Additional Information**
1. Check "Yes" or "No" for previous visits to the country
2. Answer questions about criminal record
3. Indicate any health issues
4. Add any additional notes or comments
5. Review all entered information
6. Click "Submit Application"

**Note:**
- All information must be accurate and match your passport
- False information may result in visa rejection
- Keep a copy of your application reference number

### Uploading Visa Documents

**Description:** Submit required documents for your visa application.

**How to use:**
1. After submitting the application form, you'll be directed to the Documents page
2. Review the list of required documents:
   - Passport copy (bio page)
   - Recent passport-sized photo
   - Proof of accommodation
   - Flight itinerary
   - Bank statements (last 3-6 months)
   - Employment letter (if applicable)
   - Travel insurance
3. For each document:
   - Click "Choose File" or "Upload" button
   - Select the file from your computer
   - Supported formats: PDF, JPG, PNG
   - Maximum file size: 5MB per file
4. Wait for upload confirmation (green checkmark)
5. Review uploaded documents
6. Click "Continue to Payment" when all documents are uploaded

**Note:**
- Ensure all documents are clear and legible
- Photos must meet specific requirements (white background, recent, etc.)
- Keep original documents for your records

### Tracking Visa Application

**Description:** Monitor the status of your submitted visa application.

**How to use:**
1. Go to Visa > Tracking from the main menu
2. Enter your application reference number
3. Enter your email address used in the application
4. Click "Track Application"
5. View application status:
   - **Submitted:** Application received
   - **Under Review:** Being processed by visa office
   - **Additional Documents Requested:** More information needed
   - **Approved:** Visa granted
   - **Rejected:** Application declined
6. Check for any messages or requests from the visa office
7. Download approved visa when available

### Visa Consultation Service

**Description:** Get expert help with your visa application.

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to Visa > Consultation
2. Read about the consultation services offered
3. Select consultation type:
   - Basic: Document review
   - Standard: Application assistance
   - Premium: Complete application support
4. Choose preferred consultation method:
   - Video call
   - Phone call
   - In-person meeting
5. Select available date and time slot
6. Fill in your contact information and visa requirements
7. Add specific questions or concerns
8. Click "Book Consultation"
9. Make payment for the service
10. You'll receive confirmation with meeting details

## Booking Management

### Viewing Booking Confirmation

**Description:** Access your booking confirmation immediately after completing a reservation.

**How to use:**
1. After successful payment, you'll see the confirmation page displaying:
   - Booking confirmation number
   - Service details (flight/hotel/car/activity)
   - Travel dates
   - Guest/passenger information
   - Payment confirmation
   - Total amount paid
2. Click "Download Confirmation" to save a PDF copy
3. Click "Email Confirmation" to resend to your email
4. Click "Print" to print the confirmation
5. Note down your booking reference number for future reference

### Completing Payment

**Description:** Process payment for your booking using various payment methods.

**How to use:**
1. On the Checkout or Payment page, review your booking summary
2. Select payment method:
   - **Credit/Debit Card:** Enter card details (number, expiry, CVV)
   - **Wallet:** Pay using your Wanderlust wallet balance
   - **Bank Transfer:** Follow instructions for transfer
   - **PayPal:** Click to authorize PayPal payment
   - **Other payment gateways** as available
3. Apply voucher or promo code:
   - Click "Apply Voucher"
   - Enter voucher code or select from saved vouchers
   - Click "Apply" to see discount reflected
4. Review final price after discounts
5. Check the box to agree to terms and cancellation policy
6. Click "Confirm Payment" or "Pay Now"
7. Complete any additional verification (OTP, 3D Secure, etc.)
8. Wait for payment processing confirmation

**Note:**
- Keep your payment receipt for records
- Some payment methods may have processing fees
- Failed payments will return you to the payment page

### Cancelling a Booking

**Description:** Request cancellation for a reservation and potential refund.

**How to use:**
1. Go to Profile > Booking History
2. Find the booking you want to cancel
3. Check if the booking is eligible for cancellation (status must be "Upcoming")
4. Click the "Cancel Booking" button
5. Review the cancellation policy showing:
   - Cancellation deadline
   - Refund percentage based on timing
   - Expected refund amount
   - Processing fees (if any)
6. Select cancellation reason from dropdown
7. Add additional comments explaining cancellation (optional)
8. Read and accept the cancellation terms
9. Click "Confirm Cancellation"
10. Wait for cancellation confirmation
11. Refund will be processed according to the policy
12. Check your wallet or original payment method for refund

**Note:**
- Non-refundable bookings cannot be cancelled for a refund
- Cancellation fees may apply based on timing
- Refund processing time varies (typically 7-14 business days)

### Requesting a Refund

**Description:** Submit a refund request for eligible bookings.

**How to use:**
1. Go to Profile > Booking History
2. Find the cancelled or eligible booking
3. Click "Request Refund" button
4. Fill in the refund request form:
   - Select refund reason
   - Provide detailed explanation
   - Upload supporting documents (if applicable)
   - Select preferred refund method (Wallet/Original payment method)
5. Review refund amount calculation
6. Click "Submit Refund Request"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I modify my booking after confirmation?**
A: Contact customer support with your booking reference. Changes depend on the service provider's policy and may incur fees.

**Q: How long does it take to receive a refund?**
A: Refunds typically process within 7-14 business days to the original payment method or instantly to your wallet.

**Q: Can I book for someone else?**
A: Yes, you can enter different passenger/guest information during booking. You'll remain the booking contact.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and Wanderlust wallet balance.

**Q: How do I know if my booking is confirmed?**
A: You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after successful payment. Check your Booking History for status.

**Q: Can I cancel a non-refundable booking?**
A: Non-refundable bookings cannot be cancelled for a refund, but you may be able to modify dates (subject to fees).

**Q: How do I contact the hotel/service provider directly?**
A: Vendor contact information is available in your booking confirmation and booking details page.

**Q: Are the prices shown in my local currency?**
A: Prices are displayed in VND by default. Currency conversion options may be available in settings.

**Q: Can I use multiple vouchers on one booking?**
A: No, only one voucher code can be applied per booking.

**Q: What happens if my flight is cancelled?**
A: Contact support immediately. We'll help you rebook or process a full refund based on airline policies.
